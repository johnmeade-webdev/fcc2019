William Martin Joel (1949 - )

Billy Joel at a Madison Square Garden Performance.

Billy Joel has been a force in the music industry since 1971.

Billy's career kicked off in 1971 with the release of Cold Spring Harbor. He released new music through 1993. Since then, he has been exclusively touring. Apart from his music, he also has a passion for owning and designing boats, has opened 20th Century Cycles which "maintian[s] and augment[s]" a collection of classic motorcycles that have been blended with modern rideability and practicality.

I first saw Billy Joel perform in a face-to-face concert in Minnesota with Elton John. That singular experience motivated me to continue developing my piano playing and voice throughout high school and into today.
